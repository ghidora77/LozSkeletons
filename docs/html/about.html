About Us

Luke and Ozzy started Loz Digital after teaming up on a project involving automation in the aviation industry. We realized the great potential apply data, programming and design thinking to unique problems and uncover new ways of solving problems.

Our Process

Our approach is simple. We spend plenty of time understanding and defining the problem, we break it down into as many fundamental component parts as possible, we spend time understanding what each piece does, rebuild from the ground up to create intuitive, highly functional products. The main components of problem solving stay the same, although the techniques and tools we use will always vary.
